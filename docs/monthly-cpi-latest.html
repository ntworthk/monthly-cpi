The monthly index

Figure 1 below shows that the monthly CPI indicator tracks closely with the existing quarterly indicator. Inflation for the year to April 2023 was 6.8%, according to the monthly indicator.

The monthly index shows that the Housing group increased most over the last year – by 8.9%, shown in Figure 2.

The Food and non-alcoholic beverages group increased the next most – by 7.9% – see Figure 3.
