The monthly index

Figure 1 below shows that the monthly CPI indicator tracks closely with the existing quarterly indicator. Inflation for the year to September 2022 was 7.3%, according to the monthly indicator.

The monthly index shows that the Housing group increased most over the last year – by 10.3%, shown in Figure 2.

The Food and non-alcoholic beverages group increased the next most – by 9.6% – see Figure 3.
